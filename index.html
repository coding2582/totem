Our Story

Seventy Years on Rucker Ave

"That's not a tagline. That's just Tuesday around here."

Totem opened in 1953 when Rucker Avenue was part of old Highway 99 and a hot plate and a friendly face was all anyone needed. The neon totem sign has glowed ever since, inspired by a carved story pole that's stood on this block since the 1920s.

In 2011, Steve and Alicia took the reins from longtime owners Bliss and Joyce. They kept every recipe Everett grew up on and brought dad Wayne's smoked meats along for the ride. The chicken fried steak is still hand-breaded. The pies still come out of our oven. A lot has changed on Rucker Ave. This booth hasn't.

Made from scratch, every morningHand-breaded, baked in-house, smoked over real wood. No bags, no shortcuts.
Family owned, genuinelySteve, Alicia, and Wayne — not a franchise, not a chain. The same family every day.
